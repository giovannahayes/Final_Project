Loan Prediction Project

Objective

The lending market is a highly competetive market. The whole purpose of the lending industry is to issue loans to borrowers in exchange that they will repay the loan with interest over a stated period of time. A lender will only make a profit if a borrower pays off the loan as promised and will lose money if a borrower does not pay back the loan. Determining loan eligibilty of a potential customer can be a very tedious task. With the help of machine learning algorithms, lenders can adopt this techonolgy to help determine loan eligiblity in a quick and easy way.

The objective of our project is to develop an end to end web application that helps determine the chances of the user on getting approved for a loan
